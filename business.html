Our Business







Our Business

Consulting Offerings


Project Management

Project management is vital for high impact, time sensitive projects—those most critical to an organization’s success. At Norida, we provide professional project management services to our clients, ensuring their important projects are planned, controlled and delivered successfully. Our strength is our multi-disciplinary consultants, methods, technology and tools. We use these assets to give our clients a competitive advantage in executing their time-sensitive projects efficiently and within budget. With our highly skilled and experienced team and an outlook based firmly on industry best practices, we are well placed to assist you with projects of all sizes.



Talent & Change Management

We provide talent management services to organizations that are looking to improve their business performance through increasing employee motivation, performance, upskilling and retaining top talents. We also understand that an organization’s ability to continually evolve and improve is dependent on it’s capability to actively respond to, and adopt change, both internally and externally. Norida can assist with the identification & implementation of new concepts across people, processes and technology within an organization to help transform its operations.



Research & Impact Evaluations

We provide custom evaluations of projects, programs and organizations. This work diagnoses the critical path for success as well as potential enablers and inhibitors. Our work helps our clients to achieve sustainable models that can be replicated. We conduct desk-based & field research, and qualitative interviews combined with analysis on various research projects to help clients understand the status quo, identify potential barriers and opportunities, and to pull together insights and transform them into actions.



Supply Chain & Logistics Management

With its complexities, the supply chain & logistics activities is focused on cost effectiveness and delivery of products/services in a timely manner. Norida uses an integrated approach to optimize supply chain & logistics management model to help organizations build sustainable supply chains that prepare their business for the future of work and improve employee and customer experience. The model incorporates quality assurance and risk management methodologies which are embedded in each process, alongside this, a reporting & monitoring dashboard is provided for our clients.



Monitoring and Evaluation

Excellence in monitoring and evaluation—involving robust collection of data and rigorous analysis of these data - is critical in assessing the economic and social impact of your programmes. Across disciplines globally, Norida helps organizations and governments monitor and evaluate what they do and the results they achieve. Depending on project needs, our M&E experts employ a mixed-method approach to monitoring and evaluation, ensuring that programs address accountability, measure impact, and use best practices and tools to improve program effectiveness.



Business Operations Optimization

We leverage our expertise to refine and streamline organization’s operational processes, deploy metrics and measurement systems, and improve tool utilization. Our approach seamlessly combines our technology and management expertise to deliver customized solutions that maximizes the utilization of an organization’s time, resources, and capital, and that address all elements impacting an organization’s operations, thereby creating long term value for our clients.
