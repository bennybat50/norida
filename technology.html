Data Analytics & Business Intelligence

Business excellence is driven by rational data driven decision-making. At Norida, we harness our client’s generated data through centralized business data solutions combining technology, conceptualization and methods to provide professional insights to transform the operational ecosystem of our clients and assist them achieve their commercialization goals. Biometrics & Digital Identity Management Solutions

Norida specializes in the provision of consultancy services in biometric identity management systems, and also offer field deployment support and integration services for logical and physical access control systems. We have worked with public and private sector entities to achieve their specific biometric identity management journey from enrolment to authentication objectives.



Research & Impact Evaluations

We provide custom evaluations of projects, programs and organizations. This work diagnoses the critical path for success as well as potential enablers and inhibitors. Our work helps our clients to achieve sustainable models that can be replicated. We conduct desk-based & field research, and qualitative interviews combined with analysis on various research projects to help clients understand the status quo, identify potential barriers and opportunities, and to pull together insights and transform them into actions.



Cyber Security & Digital Risk Management

We understand the security challenges and complexities facing brands, companies and government institutions from developing online threats.
